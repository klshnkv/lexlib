Peter Steinberger is an Austrian software engineer/entrepreneur (aka @steipete) best known as the founder of PSPDFKit (a developer PDF/document SDK company, later rebranded as Nutrient). More recently he’s been building open-source AI tooling—most notably OpenClaw (formerly Clawdbot), an AI agent/framework project that blew up on GitHub and in the developer community.
