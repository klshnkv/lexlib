Julia Shaw is a German-Canadian criminal psychologist and popular science writer who specialises in false memories. Shaw has been an honorary Research Associate in Psychology at University College London since 2017 and was a contributor to Scientific American between 2015 and 2017. <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Julia_Shaw_(psychologist)" target="_blank">Wikipedia</a>.
